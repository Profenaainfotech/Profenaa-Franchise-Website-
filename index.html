Core Problem Profenaa Solves

India produces millions of graduates every year, yet employability remains a challenge. Profenaa Groups was founded to correct this imbalance.

01

Graduate Unemployment Crisis

Nearly 37% – 42% of graduates remain unemployed due to lack of industry-ready skills. Despite academic qualifications, many struggle to meet real-world job expectations and practical demands.

02

Education & Industry Mismatch

Colleges focus on theory, while companies demand real tools, automation, and hands-on experience. Rapid technology changes widen the gap, making graduates unprepared for industry roles.

03

Profenaa’s Integrated Solution

Profenaa bridges the education-to-employment gap through industry-ready training, skill-based certification programs, automation engineering solutions, and scalable franchise opportunities. By combining practical learning, technology services, and career-focused education, Profenaa helps students gain job-ready skills while enabling entrepreneurs to build sustainable education businesses.
