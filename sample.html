The Core Problem Profenaa Solves

India produces millions of graduates every year, yet employability remains a challenge. Profenaa Groups was founded to correct this imbalance.

Graduate Unemployment Crisis

Nearly 37% – 42% of graduates remain unemployed after college, not due to lack of education, but lack of industry-ready skills.

Mismatch Between Education & Industry

Traditional education emphasizes theory, while industries demand practical exposure, tools, automation, and real-world experience.

Profenaa’s Integrated Solution

Profenaa Groups bridges this gap through industry-aligned curriculum, advanced skill development, automation services, and scalable franchise ecosystems.
