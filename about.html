Dr. M. Muthuraj, Ph.D.

Managing Director, Profenaa Groups

Visionary Leader & Educator Empowering Students & Entrepreneurs

Dr. Muthuraj M is the driving force behind Profenaa Groups, with over 20 years of experience in engineering education, design, and skill development. His mission is to nurture students with cutting-edge technical knowledge, hands-on project experience, and industry-ready mindset.

Under his leadership, Profenaa Groups has expanded rapidly, offering top-tier training programs and creating India’s fastest-growing education ecosystem. His commitment ensures that learners not only gain knowledge but are also ready for real-world challenges.

Beyond education, Dr. Muthuraj encourages entrepreneurship. He has designed a unique franchise model that allows partners across India to establish Profenaa centers, providing students with access to premium courses while creating business opportunities for franchise owners.

His vision is to transform India into a global hub of technological excellence by 2030, blending innovation, education, and empowerment for individuals and society at large.
